# Constraints

Active limitations that materially affect design or system behavior.

---

**Time window: end-of-experience only.**
Even exists after the experience, while people are still together, before everyone disperses. Do not expand earlier into planning or live tracking.

- ❌ Before the experience (planning, reservations, budgeting)
- ❌ During the experience (live tracking, running totals)
- ✅ Only the end-of-experience window

This constraint is intentional and defines the product.

---

**Nostalgia is not a social layer.**
The nostalgic recognition felt during allocation is a byproduct of the moment, not a stored feature. Receipts may be revisitable, but Even is not a memory archive or social network.

- ❌ Feeds, comments, likes, social timelines
- ❌ Persistent social interaction beyond the bill moment

---

**Even is not a debt tracker.**
Persistence of `Person` entities is for recall, not bookkeeping. No `Balance`, `Debt`, or `Settlement` entities. No aggregate "X owes you Y" UX, even where data could support it.

---

**Even is not a payment processor (for now).**
No payment infrastructure. No wallet. No bank-like interface. Payment intent over payment execution, when the time comes — integrations over ownership.
